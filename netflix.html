Netflix is an American entertainment company that offers online streaming services. Founded in 1997 by Reed Hastings and Marc Randolph, Netflix began as a DVD rental service by mail, but it gained fame for its online streaming platform launched in 2007. Netflix's business model revolves around the continuous streaming of content such as movies, TV series, documentaries, and other programs over the internet. Subscribers can access Netflix content through streaming apps on various devices, including televisions, computers, smartphones, and tablets. One of Netflix's distinctive features is its production of original content, including exclusive TV shows and films often referred to as "Netflix Originals." The company invests heavily in creating new content to attract and retain subscribers.
